About Me

My name is Kevin Halstead and I am currently working as city route planner for Estes Express Lines in Aurora, Colorado. I am aspiring to become a web developer and am currently taking the DU coding boot camp in Denver, Colorado. I am fueled with passion to grow and develop my skills in web development. I consider myself a forever student and I'm looking forward to growing as a developer and learning new skills and technologies.

I can attribute a plethora of obtained skills to my years in trucking and medical industries. Skills including supervising roles, managing large teams and multiple task at the same time, independent and teamwork experiences and the ability to adapt to unexpected changes as well as emergency situations to name a few.

I am very self motivated and driven to succeed. The main driving force behind everything I do is my beautiful wife and three children. They mean the world to me and challenge me to be a better version of myself everyday.
